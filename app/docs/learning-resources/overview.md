# Learning Resources

Books, courses, certifications, and sites for learning Bitcoin and Lightning development. This site’s [docs](/docs) and [Tools](/tools) cover protocol and scripting; the links below point to deeper or structured learning.

## Notes & Docs

- **[Learn Me A Bitcoin](https://learnmeabitcoin.com)** – Technical explanations and interactive tools.
- **[Bitcoin.org – Developer Documentation](https://developer.bitcoin.org)** – Guides and reference.
- **[Bitcoin Optech](https://bitcoinops.org)** – Upgrades, scaling, and operator topics.

## Books

- **[Programming Bitcoin](https://github.com/jimmysong/programmingbitcoin)** – Build Bitcoin tooling from scratch (Python).
- **[Mastering Bitcoin](https://github.com/bitcoinbook/bitcoinbook)** – Andreas Antonopoulos; free online.

## Courses & Training

- **[Bitcoin Dev Project](https://bitcoindevproject.com)** – Courses and certifications for developers.
- **[Satoshi Nakamoto Institute](https://nakamotoinstitute.org)** – Essays and historical papers.

## Certifications

- **[Bitcoin Professional Certification](https://bitcoindevproject.com)** – Developer-focused certification (when offered).
- Other programs appear periodically; check Bitcoin conferences and training providers.

## More

- **[BitcoinDev.org – Education](https://bitcoindev.org/#education)** – Broader list of guides, courses, and certs.
- **[BitcoinDev Glossary](/docs/glossary)** – Terms and definitions used in this site.
