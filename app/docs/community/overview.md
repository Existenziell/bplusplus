# Community

Podcasts, conferences, meetups, discussions, contribution, funding, and jobs in the Bitcoin and Lightning ecosystem.

## Discussions

- **[bitcoin-dev](https://lists.linuxfoundation.org/mailman/listinfo/bitcoin-dev)** – Mailing list for protocol and development.
- **[Lightning-dev](https://lists.linuxfoundation.org/mailman/listinfo/lightning-dev)** – Lightning protocol and implementation.
- **[Bitcoin Stack Exchange](https://bitcoin.stackexchange.com)** – Q&A for developers and users.

## Podcasts

- **[Bitcoin Optech](https://bitcoinops.org/en/podcast/)** – Weekly technical summary.
- **[Stephan Livera Podcast](https://stephanlivera.com)** – Bitcoin and Lightning interviews and topics.
- **[What Bitcoin Did](https://www.whatbitcoindid.com)** – Interviews and ecosystem coverage.

## Conferences & Meetups

- **[Bitcoin Core Dev Seminar](https://bitcoincore.dev)** – Technical workshops and seminars.
- **[Adopting Bitcoin](https://adoptingbitcoin.org)** – Annual conference for builders.
- Local meetups: search for “Bitcoin meetup” or “Lightning meetup” in your city.

## Contribution

- **[Bitcoin Core – Contributing](https://github.com/bitcoin/bitcoin/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md)** – How to contribute to Bitcoin Core.
- **[Bitcoin Optech – Contributing](https://bitcoinops.org/en/contribute/)** – Writing and reviewing technical content.
- **[BIPs](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips)** – Bitcoin Improvement Proposals; review and propose changes.

## Funding & Jobs

- **[Bitcoin Dev Project – Jobs](https://bitcoindevproject.com)** – Developer jobs (when listed).
- **[OpenSats](https://opensats.org)** – Grants for open-source Bitcoin projects.
- **[HRF](https://hrf.org)** – Human Rights Foundation; Bitcoin and freedom technology grants.
- Job boards and Twitter/X: search “Bitcoin developer” and “Lightning developer” for roles.

For a broader directory, see [BitcoinDev.org – Community](https://bitcoindev.org/#community).
