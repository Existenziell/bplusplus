# Bitcoin Historical Milestones

## Overview

This timeline covers major milestones in Bitcoin's history from its creation in 2009 to present day.

## Historical Milestones

### 2009
- **January 3**: Genesis Block mined
- **January 12**: First Bitcoin transaction (Satoshi to Hal Finney)
- **October 5**: First Bitcoin exchange rate published ($1 = 1,309.03 BTC)

### 2010
- **May 22**: First real-world transaction - Laszlo Hanyecz paid 10,000 BTC for two pizzas
- **July 17**: Mt. Gox exchange launched
- **August 15**: Critical bug discovered and fixed (value overflow)

### 2011
- **February 9**: Bitcoin reaches parity with USD ($1/BTC)
- **June 8**: Bitcoin reaches $31/BTC
- **November 28**: First Bitcoin halving (Block 210,000)

### 2012
- **September 27**: Bitcoin Foundation established

### 2013
- **March 28**: Market cap reaches $1 billion
- **November 29**: Bitcoin reaches $1,000/BTC

### 2016
- **July 9**: Second Bitcoin halving (Block 420,000)

### 2017
- **August 1**: Bitcoin Cash hard fork
- **December 17**: Bitcoin reaches all-time high of ~$20,000

### 2020
- **May 11**: Third Bitcoin halving (Block 630,000)

### 2024
- **April 20**: Fourth Bitcoin halving (Block 840,000)
